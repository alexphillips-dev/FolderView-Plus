Place third-party icon folders here.

Structure:
  /usr/local/emhttp/plugins/folderview.plus/images/third-party-icons/<folder-name>/<icon-file>

Example:
  /usr/local/emhttp/plugins/folderview.plus/images/third-party-icons/arr/sonarr.png
  /usr/local/emhttp/plugins/folderview.plus/images/third-party-icons/arr/radarr.png

Supported extensions:
  png, jpg, jpeg, gif, webp, svg, bmp, ico, avif
